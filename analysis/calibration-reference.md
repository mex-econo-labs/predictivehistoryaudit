## Status Definitions

- **confirmed**: Prediction has clearly come true
- **partially_confirmed**: Core direction correct but details wrong, or only part of the prediction materialized
- **disconfirmed**: Prediction clearly falsified by events
- **untested**: Not yet testable or insufficient time has passed
- **unfalsifiable**: Cannot be tested empirically
